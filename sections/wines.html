02 — ROSÉ Nereides

40 Milia Konda
2025

Athiri · Mandilaria

Story

40 nautical miles — the distance from Nisyros to the nearest mainland coast. That is how far you are from anything ordinary when you drink this wine.

Winemaking

Mavrothiriko and Assyrtiko from old dry-farmed terraces. Extended maceration, gentle extraction, aged in used oak.

Tasting Notes

Iron, dark cherry, volcanic dust. Firm tannins and a long, mineral-driven finish.

Athiri · Amphora White

Story

The warm air that breathes through the cracks of the volcano is called Apyri. This wine takes its name from that breath.

Winemaking

Athiri, part skin-contact, rested in amphora. Unclarified, unfiltered. Wild ferment, no additions.

Tasting Notes

Golden amber with copper glints. Beeswax, dried herbs, white peach. Textured palate with saline finish.
